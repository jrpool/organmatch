About OrganMatch

Your patient’s life depends on you—and luck

OrganMatch is a turn-based game for 3 to 8 players.

You and your fellow surgeons have patients needing organ transplants. There are not enough organs, so some patients will not survive the wait.

Organs and patients belong to categories. An organ matches a patient only if the patient needs that organ and they belong to the same category.

Patients differ in priority.

Patients differ in how long they have been waiting.

You will discover some facts about patients that you can use to increase or decrease their priorities.

You will have opportunities to change the patients you are caring for.

Whether your patients live or die will depend on them, on your skill in collegial decisionmaking, and on the luck of the draw.
